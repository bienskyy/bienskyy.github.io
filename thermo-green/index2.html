WHO WE ARE

WE STARTED SMALL

In September 30, 2014, DTI registered as Thermo Green Enterprise, a small entrepreneurial company that started out as distributor of instantaneous water heaters from a local water heater importer here in the country. And in September 9, 2016 Thermo Green was registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission and called as Thermo Green Water Heater Company.

WE'VE EXPANDED

Since then, Thermo Green expanded its product roster and became a strong contender in the field of water heater distribution, through steadfast commitment to seeking new suppliers with new technology and innovative engineering design and techniques.

WE TAKE PRIDE IN OUR PRESTIGE

Thermo Green’s head was able to get some prestigious projects during his previous employment and career history. Some of these land mark projects include Philippine Arena in Bulacan, One Shangrila Place in Ortigas Center, and One Eastwood Avenue,. Libis Quezon City to name a few.
